We’re looking forward to sharing the new FSAFEDS website, launching at 9 a.m. Eastern Time on Thursday, September 1, 2016.

The website has been completely refreshed – making it simple to find the information you need. And when you log in to your account for the first time on or after September 1, you’ll find all new tools and resources that make it easier than ever to manage your account.

As a reminder, you may resume submitting claims at 9 a.m. Eastern Time on September 1, 2016. Processing of Paperless Reimbursement (PR) claims and pending dependent care claims will resume on Thursday, September 8, 2016.

If you receive FSAFEDS emails at a personal email address, please be sure to add wageworks.com to your safe senders list. This will help ensure you continue to receive emails about the FSAFEDS program.

The Federal Flexible Spending Account Program (FSAFEDS) is sponsored by the U.S. Office of Personnel Management and administered by WageWorks, Inc.
